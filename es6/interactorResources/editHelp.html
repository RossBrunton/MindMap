General Help:
  • Click and drag to move nodes
  • Click and drag the green spot on an arrow to reshape it
  • Click a thing or a connecting arrow to edit it
  • Drag from the red dot on a node to create a new arrow
  • Double click on an empty space to create a new thing
  • To resize your drawing space, go to the edge and click an arrow
  • To select multiple nodes, hold shift and click them, or drag with the right mouse button
Keyboard Shortcuts:
  • CTRL+A: Select All
With Multiple Nodes Selected:
  • Escape or click anywhere: Clear selection
  • Delete: Delete selection
